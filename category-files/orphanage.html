Helping With Passion

Across Gujarat, numerous organizations are dedicated to providing love, care, and support to children without families. These orphanages offer not just shelter, but hope—helping young lives grow through education, nourishment, and emotional care. Explore some of these amazing homes below and make a difference by supporting their cause.

Shaksham Foundation

A leading NGO in Ahmedabad supporting orphans, elders, women, and underprivileged communities with food, education, medical support, and shelter.

We aim to achieve bigger goals by educating the underprivileged children and youth of our society to create a strong foundation for their future.
With proper education support and soft skill training- we can push these children to enter the mainstream of life and stand tall as dignified individuals in the upcoming time.

We are thankful to the donors for the funds and our workers who successfully conduct the programs. With their aid, they help us feed more children at the orphanage in Ahmedabad and many other children belonging to the underprivileged communities dwelling in slums.

Shaksham Foundation stands to serve humans with a humane approach.

Visamo Kids Foundation

A shelter home providing free education for underprivileged kids from across Gujarat, aiming to uplift their lives through quality education.

Visamo Kids Foundation has created a facility that can house 100+ kids with the mission to transform the lives of less privileged yet high potential kids through formal K-12 education in English (from classes I to XII) in partnership with the finest schools under CBSE, ICSE and State board.

Jagiri Home

Focused on orphanage support, child rights, education, old age support, and women empowerment, striving to create positive societal change.
We have Total 3 Children Homes. Our main Children’s Home is in Jagiri. We have a shelter home for 160 underprivileged, orphan & semi-orphan children, between the age group of 5-14 years. 94 of them are orphans. We have children from Gujarat, Maharashtra and Daman & Dadranagar Haveli.

We provide three nutritious meals each day as well as evening snacks along with morning and evening tea. Meals are eaten as a family in our multipurpose hall (We do not have a dedicated dining hall) where children, staff and volunteers come together. We emphasize on providing an environment that enables them to regain their childhood and prepares them for a better future.

Our Primary focus is to show them that we care and we want to bring them up to be aware of the changing world around them. Our vision is to make education accessible to all. We aim to equip the marginalized by deploying strategic initiatives, capacity building, and forging solidarity, thus facilitating self-confidence and self-reliance.

Parivar Mandir - Muni Seva Ashram

Provides a family-like environment for children without caregivers, ensuring they receive proper upbringing, care, and facilities.
Our philosophy is all-inclusive and aims to serve, strengthen and sustain the well being of the less fortunate and build organizational resilience through agriculture, health, education, welfare programs and alternative energy solutions. We strive to uphold our philosophy by constantly introducing new technologies on our campus and transparently utilizing our funds to ensure the betterment of the society as a whole.

Today, sustainable way of living is a way of life here at the Ashram that stands as a sprawling testament to our mother's vision. We not only love nature and live amid it, but have also incorporated methods like organic farming, agro forestry, horticulture, animal husbandry, solar energy, and biogas generation as a means to fuel our strides in being self-sufficient in a sustainable manner.

Education at our Ashram began when we began teaching girls till 10th grade in order to help them find work. Since then, we have increased our scope of endeavors in the education sector manifolds by introducing several programs that will help not only the tribal communities but also aspiring students in the realm of nursing and medicine.
