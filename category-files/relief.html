Reaching With Urgency

When disaster strikes, HaloHaven stands as a beacon of hope—bringing together hearts and hands to respond with speed and sincerity. From natural calamities to crisis zones, our relief centers provide emergency aid, shelter, food, and medical support when it’s needed most. Every donation, every helping hand, fuels a movement of compassion—because in the face of chaos, we rise with unity and rebuild with love.

Gujarat Relief Foundation

The Gujarat Relief Foundation has been providing disaster response and humanitarian aid since 2001. From earthquake recovery to cyclone relief and pandemic aid, the NGO is known for its rapid response and collaboration with local authorities to serve affected communities.
