Disaster Relief NGOs near Nadiad

When natural disasters strike, these organizations step in with emergency aid, food, shelter, and rebuilding support. Below are reliable NGOs around Nadiad working to protect and empower affected communities. Join hands with them to make a real difference.

Gujarat Relief Foundation

The Gujarat Relief Foundation has been providing disaster response and humanitarian aid since 2001. From earthquake recovery to cyclone relief and pandemic aid, the NGO is known for its rapid response and collaboration with local authorities to serve affected communities.

Akshaya Patra Relief Services

During floods, earthquakes, and the COVID-19 pandemic, Akshaya Patra has consistently stepped up with meal distribution, ration kits, and relief supplies to vulnerable populations. Their field teams operate kitchens across Gujarat for mass food relief.

Helping Hands Gujarat

Helping Hands is a youth-led volunteer NGO dedicated to disaster preparedness and recovery. They work closely with healthcare workers and emergency responders to deliver relief items, evacuate affected individuals, and rebuild homes in rural Gujarat.
